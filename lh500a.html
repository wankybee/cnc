Design

High Rigidity! Exception Stabilty! Equipped with automatic pallet change.

  • The major structural parts are manufactured form Meehanite cast iron, hardened to hardness over HB190
  • The structural parts are tempered for stress relief that ensures deformation-free performance year after year.
  • X, Y, Z axis are mounted with roller type linear ways, exhibiting extra high rigidity and loading capacity.
  • Oversized column structure dramatically increases machining stability.
  • Fixed column construction.
